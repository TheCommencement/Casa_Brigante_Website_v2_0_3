About

One name. One standard.

Casa Brigantè is the public institution of The Commencement, bringing together books, music, audio productions, and original brands under a single identity.

The rule of the catalog is simple: nothing is made that is not meant to be kept, and nothing kept is ever retired. Work arrives when it is finished. It stays in the order it arrived. The record is permanent by design.

The house is founded, written, and produced by Aries Brigantè, one title at a time. The rest of his story is told the way the house prefers: in the work.

The building is quiet. The work is not.

The institution

The structure behind the name.

Behind Casa Brigantè stands The Commencement, the holding institution, and the working entities that support each discipline: Excelsa Cornua Rights Group for publishing and rights, Ascendant Soundworks for audio production, Nino & Russia Brown Creative Studio, La Finca Management, and the legacy imprints TFG Publications and TaxxFree Enterprises.

Visitors meet the work. The structure carries it.
