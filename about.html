About

One name. One standard.

Casa Brigantè is the public institution of The Commencement, bringing together books, music, audio productions, and original brands under a single identity.

The rule of the catalog is simple: nothing is made that is not meant to be kept, and nothing kept is ever retired. Work arrives when it is finished. It stays in the order it arrived. The record is permanent by design.

The building is quiet. The work is not.
