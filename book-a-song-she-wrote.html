A Song She Wrote

Some songs become hits. Others become history.

Solana never wanted attention. She wanted the music to speak for itself. A brilliant producer and songwriter hiding in plain sight, she’s spent years perfecting her craft while the world rewards louder voices with less talent.

Ahmad has the hunger, the lyrics, and the dream, but talent alone has never been enough.

When a chance encounter at an underground open mic brings them together, they begin chasing something bigger than fame. As success, love, ambition, and personal demons collide, they’ll discover that the songs people remember most often come from the pain they never wanted to survive.

A Song She Wrote is a raw, emotionally charged novel about music, purpose, mental health, and what it truly costs to leave your mark on the world.
