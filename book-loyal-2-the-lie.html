Loyal 2 the Lie

Talent can change your life. The wrong people can change everything else.

Raised in the streets of West Palm Beach, Tariq Harris Jr. has spent his entire life chasing an opportunity few people ever get. As his rise from overlooked prospect to football superstar captures the nation’s attention, fame, fortune, and limitless opportunity finally seem within reach.

But success attracts more than admiration.

Behind the cameras, endorsements, and headlines lies a world where ambition is exploited, loyalty is tested, and trust becomes the most dangerous gamble of all. With every victory pulling him further from the life he once knew, Tariq is forced to confront a painful question: when the people closest to you become your greatest threat, who do you trust?

Loyal 2 the Lie is a gripping novel about family, fame, betrayal, manipulation, and the devastating consequences of staying loyal to the wrong lie.
