October's Change

Some futures can’t be built until the past is finally confronted.

Twenty-one years after walking away from college, October Greene returns to finish the degree she never got and chase the career she’s worked her entire life to earn. But stepping back onto campus means reopening old wounds she spent decades trying to bury.

As buried truths resurface, fractured family relationships are tested, and the weight of choices made long ago refuses to stay in the past, October finds herself fighting for far more than a diploma. She’s fighting for forgiveness, for identity, and for the chance to finally become the woman she was always meant to be.

October’s Change is a deeply emotional novel about ambition, motherhood, friendship, redemption, and discovering that it’s never too late to reclaim your life.
