Studio

Drama for the ear, performed in the dark.

The audio wing of the house: serialized fiction written to be heard, produced under Ascendent Soundworks.

The practice

Precision in every frequency.

Every production is written, voiced, and mixed to one standard: the listener should forget they are listening. No narration that explains what the scene already said. No sound that exists to impress rather than to serve.

The first productions arrive when they are ready.
